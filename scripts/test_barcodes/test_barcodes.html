Linking Barcodes to Medicines

To use these test barcodes with your actual medicines:

  1. Go to Supplier Invoice or Patient Invoice
  2. Scan a barcode from this page
  3. In the "Link Barcode" modal, search for the medicine
  4. Select the medicine and click "Link & Continue"
  5. The GTIN is now saved to that medicine record

  4. Save the medicine
